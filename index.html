About Me



Welcome to my website! My name is Jessica Scott, and I am a 25-year-old animation graduate from the University of Lincoln and a self-taught illustrator. I am currently looking for work within the creative industry. I am currently working on a personal project to help build up my portfolio, and help develop my skills in the field. My favourite aspects of the production process are designing characters, drawing up storyboards and creating compelling stories. I like to experiment with texture and interesting environment concepts. While I believe my core strengths are in illustration, I still enjoy animating and am always trying to improve. My hobbies include; Playing my instruments, socializing with my friends, playing games, drawing (digitally and traditionally), animation, Anything paranormal and looking at cool creatures within cryptozoology and mythology!
